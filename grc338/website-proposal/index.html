Introduction

Essentials the Label

Essentials is a sustainable clothing store that sells high-quality wardrobe staples.

Target audience

The target audience are environmentally-conscious young and middle-aged women. Since sustainable clothing is more expensive due to the higher production cost, the demographic will mostly be people with higher incomes.

The primary goal of the audience is to build a more sustainable wardrobe and stop consuming fast fashion. They are looking to buy basic pieces that are easy to style and will last a long time.

About

Our mission is to limit waste by creating pieces with lasting fabrics and sustainable practices. Our clothes are meant to stand the test of time, both in quality and style. To achieve this, we use classic color palettes, simple designs and don't follow short-lived trends. Most of our pieces can be styled together, making them easy to incorporate into any outfit.
